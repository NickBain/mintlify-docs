## Overview

Share your roadmap with stakeholders by exporting it as an image or PDF. Choose between Timeline and List view formats depending on your audience.

## Export Formats

### Image Export (PNG)

- High-resolution image of your roadmap
- Perfect for presentations and documents
- Available in Timeline and List view formats

### PDF Export

- Multi-page document for comprehensive sharing
- Printable format with clear formatting
- Available in Timeline and List view formats

## View-Specific Exports

### Timeline Format

  <img src="/images/placeholder-export-timeline.png" alt="Timeline Export" />
  {/* TODO: Add screenshot of timeline export preview */}
</Frame>

Exports your roadmap in a Gantt-style layout:

- Items displayed on a timeline
- Grouped by your current grouping setting
- Date range and zoom level preserved

Best for:
- Quarterly/annual planning presentations
- Stakeholder updates
- Visual roadmap sharing

### List Format

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/placeholder-export-list.png" alt="List Export" />
  {/* TODO: Add screenshot of list export preview */}
</Frame>

Exports items in a structured list:

- Hierarchical item display
- All item details visible
- Sorted and grouped per your settings

Best for:
- Detailed project documentation
- Sprint planning documents
- Complete item inventories

## Exporting Your Roadmap

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure Your View">
    Set filters, grouping, and date range to show exactly what you want to export.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Export Menu">
    Click the **Export** button in the toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Format">
    Choose your export format:
    - **Image (Timeline)** - PNG of timeline view
    - **Image (List)** - PNG of list view
    - **PDF (Timeline)** - PDF of timeline view
    - **PDF (List)** - PDF of list view
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download">
    Your export is generated and downloaded automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Export Settings

Before exporting, you can configure:

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Date Range** | Which time period to include |
| **Include Completed** | Whether to show completed items |
| **Item Details** | Level of detail shown (name only, or with dates/status) |

## Item Information

Exported items display:

- Item name
- Item type badge (color-coded)
- Date range (start - end)
- Strategic priority (if assigned)
- Status indicator

<Tip>
  Apply filters before exporting to create focused views for specific audiences.
</Tip>

## File Naming

Exported files are named automatically:

- `roadmap-timeline-2024-01-20.png`
- `roadmap-list-2024-01-20.pdf`

The date reflects when the export was created.

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Export for Your Audience">
    Use Timeline format for visual stakeholders, List format for detail-oriented teams.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Apply Filters First">
    Filter to relevant items before exporting to avoid overwhelming your audience.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use PDF for Documentation">
    PDFs work better for formal documentation and email attachments.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Regular Updates">
    Schedule regular exports (weekly/monthly) to keep stakeholders informed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
